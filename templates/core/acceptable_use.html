Acceptable use policy

Last updated: December 2024

This acceptable use policy governs the use of Kairos by all users, including Clients and Experts. By using our platform, you agree to comply with this policy.

1. Permitted use

Kairos is designed to facilitate legitimate professional consultations between verified experts and clients seeking knowledge and advice. Permitted uses include:

  • Seeking professional advice and guidance
  • Research consultation and methodology review
  • Career mentoring and development
  • Technical knowledge transfer
  • Industry expertise and insights
  • Project consultation and feedback

2. Prohibited activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on our platform:

Academic dishonesty

  • Ghostwriting: Writing academic papers, theses, dissertations, or assignments for clients to submit as their own work
  • Contract cheating: Completing assessed work for students to submit under their name
  • Exam assistance: Providing answers or assistance during examinations
  • Plagiarism facilitation: Helping clients submit work that is not their own

Fraudulent activities

  • Misrepresenting qualifications or credentials
  • Providing false information during verification
  • Creating fake reviews or testimonials
  • Using stolen payment methods

Harmful content

  • Sharing illegal, harmful, or offensive material
  • Harassment, abuse, or threatening behaviour
  • Discrimination based on protected characteristics
  • Sharing confidential or proprietary information without authorisation

Platform abuse

  • Attempting to circumvent platform fees
  • Soliciting direct payments outside the platform
  • Creating multiple accounts to evade restrictions
  • Automated scraping or data collection

3. Expert responsibilities

Experts must:

  • Provide accurate information about their qualifications
  • Deliver consultations professionally and ethically
  • Respect client confidentiality
  • Decline requests that violate this policy
  • Report suspected violations to Kairos

4. Client responsibilities

Clients must:

  • Use consultations for legitimate purposes only
  • Not request services that violate this policy
  • Respect expert boundaries and expertise
  • Provide accurate information about their needs

5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Warning and education
  • Temporary suspension of account
  • Permanent account termination
  • Forfeiture of earnings (for Experts)
  • Reporting to relevant authorities or academic institutions

6. Reporting violations

If you become aware of any activity that violates this policy, please report it immediately to abuse@kairos.example.com. All reports are treated confidentially.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as needed. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
