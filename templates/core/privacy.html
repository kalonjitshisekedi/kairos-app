Privacy policy

Last updated: December 2024

This privacy policy explains how Kairos ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile information: Professional details, qualifications, expertise areas (for Experts)
  • Verification documents: Academic certificates, employment letters (for Experts)
  • Consultation data: Booking details, session notes, attachments, reviews
  • Payment information: Transaction records (payment details are processed by Stripe)
  • Usage data: Login times, pages visited, features used

2. Purpose of processing

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our consultation services
  • To verify expert credentials
  • To facilitate bookings and payments
  • To communicate with you about your account and services
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary to fulfil our agreement with you
  • Legal obligation: Where required by law
  • Legitimate interest: Where we have a legitimate business reason

4. Data minimisation

We only collect personal information that is necessary for the purposes described in this policy. We do not collect unnecessary data.

5. Data sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Other users: Your profile information is shared with other users as necessary for consultations
  • Service providers: Third-party services that help us operate (e.g., Stripe for payments, email providers)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law or legal process

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Employee training on data protection

7. Data retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Your rights

Under POPIA and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw previously given consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us or use the account deletion request feature.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate our platform. These cookies are necessary for security and functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

10. International transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Children's privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the platform.

13. Contact us

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your personal information:

Email: privacy@kairos.example.com
